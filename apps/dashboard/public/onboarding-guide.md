# Appalix Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Appalix! This guide walks you through everything you need to know to set up your account, invite your team, and start managing leads — step by step.

---

## Step 1: Set Up Your Profile

When you first log in, your name may not be set yet.

**To add your name:**
1. Look at the left sidebar. You will see a small circle with a letter in it — that is your avatar.
2. Hover over the sidebar so it expands.
3. You will see your email address and a small pencil icon next to it.
4. Click the pencil icon. This takes you to the **Edit Profile** page.
5. Type in your first name and last name.
6. Click **Save changes**.

Your name will now appear in the sidebar and in the team member list.

---

## Step 2: Set Up Your Business Profile

Your business profile helps the Appalix AI understand who you are and what you sell, so it can give smarter answers to your customers.

**To set it up:**
1. Go to **Settings** (click the gear icon in the left sidebar).
2. Scroll to the **Business Profile** section at the top.
3. Fill in:
   - **Company name** — the name of your business
   - **Industry** — e.g. Retail, SaaS, Consulting
   - **What you sell** — list your main products or services
   - **Who your customers are** — e.g. small business owners, homeowners
4. You can also paste your website URL and Appalix will try to fill these in for you automatically.
5. Click **Save**.

---

## Step 3: Invite Your Team

You can invite colleagues to join your workspace. Each person gets a **role** that controls what they can see and do.

### What the roles mean:

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything. Only one per workspace. |
| **Admin** | Same as owner except cannot delete the workspace. |
| **Manager** | Can invite and manage employees. Can assign leads. Can see all team activity. |
| **Employee** | Standard team member. Can view and work on what they are given access to. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only. Can look but cannot make any changes. |

### How to invite someone:
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Team members** section.
3. Click **+ Invite member** (top right of that section).
4. Enter their email address.
5. Choose their role from the dropdown.
6. Click **Send invite**.

They will receive an email with a link to join. Once they accept, they will appear in the team list.

> **Note:** You can only invite people to roles below your own. For example, a Manager can invite Employees but cannot invite other Managers or Admins.

---

## Step 4: Set Permissions for Team Members

By default, employees can view contacts, pipelines, and projects. You can customise exactly what each person can access.

**To change someone's permissions:**
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Permissions** section (below Team members).
3. Click on a team member's name to expand their permissions.
4. You will see a list of toggles:
   - **View Contacts** — can they see your contacts list?
   - **View Pipelines** — can they see the sales pipelines?
   - **View Projects** — can they see the projects board?
   - **View Dashboard** — can they see the overview dashboard?
   - **Allocate Leads** — can they assign leads to other team members?
   - **Reassign Leads** — can they move a lead from one person to another?
   - **Edit Deals** — can they make changes to deals in the pipeline?
5. Toggle each one on or off. It saves automatically.

> **Tip:** Managers and above always have full access. Permissions only apply to employees and members.

---

## Step 5: View the Dashboard as a Team Member

If you are a Manager or above, you can see the dashboard exactly as one of your team members sees it — useful for checking what they can access or reviewing their workload.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to **Overview** (the dashboard page).
2. At the top you will see a bar showing your team members.
3. Click any name to switch to their view.
4. An amber banner will appear at the top saying **"Viewing as [name]"**.
5. Click **Exit view** to go back to your own dashboard.

---

## Step 6: Manage Your Leads

Leads are people who have shown interest in your business — captured from Meta Ads, Google Ads, forms, and other sources.

**To see your leads:**
1. Click **All Leads** in the left sidebar (under the Forms section).

**What you can do on the leads page:**
- **Search** for a lead by name, email, or company.
- **Filter** by platform (Meta, Google, etc.) or priority (High, Medium, Low).
- **Move to Pipeline** — click the arrow button on any lead to move them into your CRM pipeline for follow-up.
- **Assign a lead** — if you are a Manager or have allocation permission, you will see a dropdown in the **Assigned** column. Choose a team member to assign the lead to them.
- **Delete** a lead using the bin icon.

---

## Step 7: Assign Leads to Your Team (Lead Allocation)

If you manage a team, you can distribute leads so each person knows who to follow up with.

**To assign a lead:**
1. Go to **All Leads**.
2. Find the lead you want to assign.
3. In the **Assigned** column, click the dropdown that says "Unassigned".
4. Select a team member from the list.
5. The lead is now assigned to them. The system records who assigned it and when.

> **Note:** You can only assign leads to people below your role level (e.g. a Manager can assign to an Employee, but not to another Manager).

**To remove an assignment:** select "Unassigned" from the same dropdown.

---

## Step 8: Lead Distribution (Round Robin)

Instead of manually assigning each lead, you can turn on **round-robin** mode. This automatically distributes new leads equally across your accepted team members in rotation.

**To turn it on:**
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Scroll to the **Lead Distribution** section.
3. Toggle the switch to **On**.

New leads from bots, emails, and form submissions will now be automatically assigned to team members one by one in turn.

---

## Step 9: Connect Your Chatbots

Your AI chatbots capture leads and answer customer questions automatically on your website.

**To set up a bot:**
1. Click **Bots** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Create bot** and give it a name.
3. Add knowledge — documents, FAQs, or website pages — so the bot knows what to say.
4. Copy the embed code and paste it into your website.

Once embedded, the bot will start conversations, collect names and emails, and create leads automatically in Appalix.

---

## Step 10: Check Your Settings

Before going live, make sure these are set up:

| What to check | Where to find it |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Business profile is filled in | Settings → Business Profile |
| Team members have been invited | Settings → Team members |
| Permissions are correct for each person | Settings → Permissions |
| Email automation is configured (Resend) | Settings → Automation |
| Round-robin is on if you want auto-assignment | Settings → Lead Distribution |
| Billing plan is correct | Settings → Billing |

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change someone's role after they join?**
Yes. In Settings → Team members, click on a member's name. If you outrank them, you will see a role dropdown to change their role.

**Q: What happens if I remove a team member?**
They lose access immediately. Their leads and contacts stay in your workspace — nothing is deleted.

**Q: Why can't I see the Permissions section?**
Permissions are only visible to Managers and above. If you are an Employee or Viewer, you will not see this section.

**Q: What is the difference between a Member and an Employee?**
They have the same access level. "Employee" is the newer recommended name. Both can be given the same custom permissions.

**Q: How do I change the email that invites come from?**
This is set by your workspace administrator in the automation settings. By default, invites are sent from your configured sender email.
